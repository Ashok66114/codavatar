Our Company

Who we are

Codavatar is a team of enthusiastic and creative individuals. At Codavatar, we help you to validate your product idea with POC development and turn your idea into a fully working MVP. With over 10 years of experience in helping clients through
the technology journey. With “Results by Technology”, we steer continuous improvement through building and transferring skills and expertise.
Our Mission

Helping passionate founders achieve their startup dream

We aim to help startup founders from all over the world build rapid-growth products. Our clients choose us because our world-class engineers and product engineering practices help them innovate and adapt faster. We’re determined to find better ways to design, create, and validate new ideas. To foster a long-lasting impact for companies and the people they serve, we combine those learnings with an unwavering commitment to customer success.
